# Server Rules 


## 1. Be Respectful
- Treat all players with respect.
- No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
- Keep chat friendly and appropriate.


## 2. No Griefing
- Do not destroy, modify, or troll other players' builds.
- Ask permission before building right next to someone else's base.


## 3. No Stealing
- Do not take items from chests, farms, or bases that do not belong to you.
- If a chest is public property be respectful and don't take everything.


## 4. No Spamming
- Do not spam chat, commands, or advertisements.


## 5. PvP Rules
- PvP is only allowed in PvP arenas.


## 6. Avoid Lag Machines
- Large redstone machines or farms that cause lag may be removed.


## 7. Follow Staff Instructions
- Moderators and admins have the final say.
- If you have a problem, contact staff instead of arguing in chat.


## 8. Use Common Sense
- If something feels unfair or annoying, don't do it.
- Staff may act on behavior not explicitly listed here.


Have fun and enjoy playing!
